# ✅ Two New Names Added!

## 🎉 What's Been Added

### **1. Ar-Rahman (ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنُ) - The Most Merciful** 
**Number:** 1 (First of the 99 names!)

**Story Theme:** Universal mercy to all creation (9 screens)

**Screen Breakdown:**
1. **Creation** - Mercy woven into existence
2. **The Sun** - Rises for everyone, no discrimination
3. **Rain** - Falls on all lands, grateful or not
4. **Food** - Provision continues for all
5. **Animals** - Every creature receives His care
6. **Everyone** - Muslim, non-Muslim, rich, poor—all receive
7. **Every Breath** - Each heartbeat is His mercy
8. **The Lesson** - Mercy that flows freely, expecting nothing
9. **Final Reveal** - Quranic verse + connection to Ar-Rahman

**Key Message:** Allah's mercy is universal, unconditional, and endless. It reaches everyone.

**Quranic Reference:** Surah Ar-Rahman 55:1-2
**Audio File:** `ar-rahman-55-1.mp3`
**Reflection:** *"When have you witnessed Allah's mercy extended to everyone, regardless of who they are?"*

---

### **2. Al-Ghaffar (ٱلْغَفَّارُ) - The Oft-Forgiving**
**Number:** 15

**Story:** Prophet Yunus AS in the whale (10 screens)

**Screen Breakdown:**
1. **Mission** - Called his people, they refused
2. **Frustration** - Patience wore thin
3. **Leaving** - Left without Allah's permission (a mistake!)
4. **The Ship** - Boarded a vessel, trying to escape
5. **The Storm** - Ship in danger, lots drawn
6. **Swallowed** - Thrown overboard, whale swallowed him
7. **Darkness** - Three layers of darkness (night, ocean, whale)
8. **The Duaa** - "No god but You! I was of the wrongdoers"
9. **Freedom** - Forgiven, restored, released
10. **Final Reveal** - Quranic verse + connection to Al-Ghaffar

**Key Message:** No sin is too great. Allah forgives repeatedly. Turn to Him sincerely.

**Quranic Reference:** Surah As-Saffat 37:143-144
**Audio File:** `as-saffat-37-143.mp3`
**Reflection:** *"What mistakes have you made where you experienced Allah's forgiveness?"*

## 🎨 Story Unique Features

### **Ar-Rahman (Universal Mercy):**
- **Visuals:** Sun, rain, food, animals, diverse people
- **Tone:** Warm, inclusive, gentle
- **Animation:** Breathing circle, rotating sun rays, falling rain
- **Message:** Mercy without asking, mercy to all
- **Emotion:** Gratitude and humility

### **Al-Ghaffar (Repeated Forgiveness):**
- **Visuals:** Storm, whale, darkness, praying hands
- **Tone:** Dark → hopeful, mistake → redemption
- **Animation:** Rocking ship, glowing duaa
- **Message:** Return is always possible
- **Emotion:** Relief and hope
- **Unique:** Shows a prophet making a mistake (relatable!)
